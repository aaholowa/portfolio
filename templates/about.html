02 : About me

About

Welcome to my personal website! I'm Adam Holowaychuk, a fourth-year electrical engineering student with a passion for robotics, embedded systems, and hardware. I enjoy learning programming languages like Python, C++, and MATLAB and am an active member of my school's robotics club. My fascination with robotics lies in the development of embedded systems and software that enable machines to interact with the world. Overall, I'm driven to explore new frontiers in electrical engineering and excited to see where my skills and interests take me.

Hobbies

Ice Hockey: When I'm not in school, I play junior hockey for the Strathcona Bruins. I have completed 4 years of junior hockey and have over 17+ years of competitive ice hockey experience. I am now excited to begin playing senior AA with the Camrose Crush. I also enjoy giving back to my community by coaching minor hockey league teams through my local hockey association. It's a great opportunity to share my knowledge and passion for the sport with the next generation of players.

Robotics/Hardware:I am proud to be an active member of the ARVP (Autonomous Robotic Vehicle Project) club at my university, where we design and build a submarine robot named "Arctos." Through this club, I have had the opportunity to participate in exciting competitions such as RoboSub, where we showcase our robot's capabilities against those of other schools. As a passionate electrical engineering student, I joined ARVP to further enhance my hardware skills and learn how to design Printed Circuit Boards (PCBs). I attend weekly meetings on Saturdays, where I enjoy collaborating with like-minded students who share my love for robotics and electrical engineering.
